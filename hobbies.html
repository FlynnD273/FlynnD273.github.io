Hobbies

Quadcopters

A few years ago, I fell in love with first person view (FPV) quadcopters. These drones can be incredibly agile, and are super fun to fly. I've learned how to build and repair my own drones, as well as design 3D printed parts for it.

I have also helped numerous people on a Discord server with troubleshooting their FPV drones. At WPI, I have also helped teach workshops about FPV drones.

Art

I have always had an interest in 2D and 3D animation, visual effects, drawing, and photography. The majority of the software I use is free and open source.

I have extensive experience in using Blender, DaVinci Resolve, Embergen, Nuke, Natron, GIMP, Krita, and Procreate. I also have experience using Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, Animate, and Lightroom. You can find more of my projects on my Instagram page.
